Sereno

Sereno is a narrative time-loop investigation game set in 1940s post-war Madrid, where players alternate between a watchman at night and a mailman during the day. Each loop represents a full day divided into four evolving stages, pushing players to explore the city, gather Items, develop Ideas, and build Instincts to uncover clues and reconstruct the truth behind a murder tied to both characters' pasts.

WHO IS BEHIND ALL THESE?

We are Midnight Loop, a remote Spanish video game studio focused on creating narrative experiences, where the player acts and the world reacts.

Forged in chaos, we set our sights on creating stories that linger after the screen fades to black. Building hand-crafted worlds designed to breath, places that invite to belong. From the first spark of the idea to its final refined form, we chase emotions and resonance.
